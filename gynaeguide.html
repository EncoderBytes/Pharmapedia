Gynae Guide By Pharmapedia

DESCRIPTION

From Gynae Guide you can Experience practical, up-to-date calculators (i.e Period of Gestation), criteria, and classification. Gynae Guide provides on-the-spot easy and instant access to obstetricians, gynecologists, and students' answers. Cover all areas of Gynecology and Obstetrics, where you can carry out complex calculations in seconds. Extensive use of detailed tables and icons throughout this mobile application enhances the user experience and clarifies complex calculations, classifications, and criteria. In this app, you can also learn and practice checking Clinical presentations, General overview of diseases, Etiology, Pharmacological & Non-Pharmacological therapies, and much more.
